Karamar Mountains

Karamar is a mountain in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and has an elevation of 579 metres. History

The pashto famous fold story "Yousaf khan and Sherbano" tombs are situated at the top of this mountain. People do visit for hiking and seeing the tomb of yousaf khan. He would suppose to do sporting of deers and rabbits. The mountain is still green with alpine trees and monkeys are found in abundace here.
